Turn Surplus into Social Impact

WeCare is a platform that bridges the gap between corporate donors with surplus goods and the NGOs that can distribute them to communities in need.

For Donors For NGOs

How It Works

1. List Surplus

Donors easily list their surplus goods, providing details and photos.

2. Get Matched

Our platform intelligently matches listings with the needs of registered NGOs.

3. Coordinate Pickup

Donors and NGOs coordinate pickup and delivery through our secure messaging system.

4. Track Impact

Donors receive detailed reports on the impact of their contributions.
